Walter Jack Rabbit, John Whiteman and Bob Howe gardens, 1932 (8976.jpg), by CIVAM Included in district farm reports, these photographs document Crow Indians gardens, 4-H, and home economic activities in the 1930s and 1940s. The photographs are stored in the National Archives and Records Administration.
